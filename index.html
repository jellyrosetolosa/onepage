It's More Fun in The Philippines

Peaceful places

PALAWAN Wonderful island

If you have the unfortunate dilemma of choosing only one place to visit in the Philippines, Palawan should be on the top of your list. You came to the country to see spectacular beaches and islands. You’re going to experience the best in Palawan.

Cordillera Cooler place

The Philippines isn’t all about postcard-perfect beaches. It has cool highland landscapes and rich mountain culture too! The Cordilleras has many of the country’s most scenic natural landscapes, waterfalls, and mountain summits. Its famous tourist places include Banaue , Sagada, and Baguio.

Bicol wonderful Sorrounding

Want to add extraordinary photo souvenirs of nature to your collection of beach holiday selfies? In Bicol, You can find the World’s most perfect cone-shaped volcano and swim with whale sharks in addition to some of the country’s most scenic islands.
